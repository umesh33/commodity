Who we are

Commodity General Trading International was established in the 2nd quarter of 2013 and as a result of a vision in trading and distribution of specific steel products in niche markets.

Creating and adding value: that is our target and business concept, where our business model is founded.

Commodity General Trading International is operating in the steel trading and distribution activities focused on USA, Europe, China, CIS countries and Sub-Saharan Africa, with a team of skilled professionals.

The Headquarters of the Company are based in Lugano, with a network of companies, local offices, and agents operating in Germany, Italy, Spain, Poland, Turkey, North America, China and South Korea.
